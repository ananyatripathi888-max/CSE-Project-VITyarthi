Problem Statement
Due to their lack of familiarity with compound interest formulas, many people and students find it difficult to determine the future value of their loans, savings, or investments. It can be difficult to comprehend how compounding operates over various time periods, and manual computations are prone to error. The goal of this tool is to increase financial literacy and make these computations easier.

Scope of the Project
The Compound Interest Calculator supports basic and advanced compound interest calculations. Users can enter principal amount, interest rate, time duration, and select compounding frequency. The tool computes both total interest accrued and final value, and is designed to be extendable for additional features such as data visualization and export.

Target Users
•	Students learning about financial concepts or mathematics.
•	Working professionals planning investments or loans.
•	Educators who need a teaching aid for financial mathematics.
•	Anyone wanting to quickly estimate the effects of compounding.

High-Level Features
•	Accurate calculation of compound interest for variable compounding periods.
•	Input validation to ensure realistic, error-free estimates.
•	Command-line interface for simple and quick usage.
•	Visualization of compound growth over time.
•	Easily adaptable for improvements or integration into other platforms.
